Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Manhattan Beach garage cleanup take?

Most jobs complete in 4-8 hours depending on size and clutter level. A light 1-car cleanup might be done in 3 hours; a packed 3-car could take a full day. We give you a time estimate with your quote.

Can you work around my cars in the garage?

We need the garage empty of vehicles to do a proper cleanup. Most Manhattan Beach clients move cars to the driveway or street for the day. We'll let you know when it's safe to pull back in.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance service?

Our focus is the initial transformation. However, many clients book us annually for a "reset" to prevent re-accumulation. We're happy to schedule recurring cleanups.

What if I'm not sure what to keep or toss?

No problem. We work with you on decision-making. Our approach: if you haven't used it in 2+ years and it has no sentimental value, it's probably clutter. But ultimately, you decide everything.

Are you insured?

Yes. We carry liability insurance. Your property and our team are protected throughout the job.

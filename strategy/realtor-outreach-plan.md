## Risk Mitigation and Contingency Planning

### Market Entry Risks

**Risk**: Staging companies view as competition rather than partnership
**Mitigation**: Clear positioning as complementary service, revenue sharing structure

**Risk**: Luxury agents skeptical of new service provider credibility
**Mitigation**: Free consultation period, staging company endorsements, detailed case studies

**Risk**: Pricing too high for market acceptance
**Mitigation**: Tiered pricing structure, value demonstration through free consultations

### Operational Risks

**Risk**: Service delivery doesn't meet luxury market expectations
**Mitigation**: Comprehensive quality standards, regular training, customer feedback loops

**Risk**: Referral partners don't generate sufficient volume
**Mitigation**: Multiple partnership channels, direct marketing backup strategy

**Risk**: Competitive response from established players
**Mitigation**: First-mover advantage, exclusive partnerships, premium positioning

### Financial Risks

**Risk**: Consultation fees don't justify referral payouts
**Mitigation**: Conservative fee structure, performance-based adjustments

**Risk**: Long sales cycles impact cash flow
**Mitigation**: Quick consultation turnaround, immediate payment terms

**Risk**: Market proves smaller than anticipated
**Mitigation**: Geographic expansion plan, service expansion options

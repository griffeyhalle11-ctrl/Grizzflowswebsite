Complete Garage Cleanout: We empty the entire space, clean it thoroughly, and reorganize only what you want to keep. By the time we're done, you'll have a functional garage where you can actually park your car, find your tools, and access your belongings without a treasure hunt.

Heavy Item Removal: Old refrigerators, broken furniture, exercise equipment you swore you'd use—we haul it all. Gardena homes often have second fridges or freezers in the garage that stopped working years ago. We'll remove them properly, including handling refrigerant recycling requirements.

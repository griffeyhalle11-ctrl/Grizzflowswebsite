Garage Cleanup for Home Sellers

Your garage is one of the first—and last—things buyers see during a showing. A cluttered garage signals deferred maintenance and reduces perceived home value. A clean, organized garage says "this home is well-maintained" and can genuinely impact your sale price. GrizzFlow's Garage helps South Bay home sellers transform their garages before listing—typically in a single day for $350-$695.

Why Your Garage Matters When Selling

Real estate studies consistently show that buyers form impressions within seconds. Your garage is often:

  • The last thing buyers see before leaving (and making their decision)
  • A proxy for how you've maintained the rest of the home
  • Usable square footage that buyers mentally calculate

A packed, dusty garage tells buyers you've been putting off maintenance. A clean garage signals pride of ownership and attention to detail—qualities that translate to higher offers.

The Home Seller's Garage Dilemma

Meanwhile, you're facing a deadline: your listing date. You don't have time for multiple dump runs, Craigslist coordination, and organizing what's left.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon before listing should I clean the garage?

Ideally 1-2 weeks before photos are taken. This gives you time to see the space fresh and make any final adjustments. We can accommodate tighter timelines if needed—sometimes we do same-day cleanups before photographer visits.

What if I need to store items until I move?

We can help you identify what needs to go to storage and organize those items for easy transport. We don't provide storage ourselves, but we can prepare items for a storage unit or POD.

Can you coordinate with my real estate agent?

Absolutely. We're happy to work around showing schedules and coordinate directly with your agent if that's easier. Just let us know their contact info.

What if I'm selling the items, not trashing them?

We can set aside sale items and help you organize them. If you're doing an estate sale or garage sale, we can prep the space and remaining items after those sales happen.

Do you serve all of South Bay?

Yes. We cover Hermosa Beach, Manhattan Beach, Redondo Beach, Torrance, El Segundo, Palos Verdes, and surrounding areas. If you're in the South Bay, we can help.

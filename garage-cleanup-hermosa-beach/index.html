Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you clean my Hermosa Beach garage?

Most Hermosa Beach garage cleanups are completed in a single day. We typically have same-week availability, and for urgent situations, we can often accommodate next-day service.

Do you handle large items like old surfboards and beach equipment?

Absolutely. We regularly haul away surfboards, paddleboards, beach chairs, umbrellas, and all types of outdoor equipment. If it fits in your garage, we can remove it.

What happens to the junk you remove?

We sort everything. Recyclables go to recycling facilities, donatable items go to local charities, and the rest is properly disposed of at licensed facilities. We handle all of this—it's included in your flat rate.

Do I need to be home during the cleanup?

We recommend being present at the start so you can walk us through what to keep and what to remove. After that, you're free to go about your day. Many Hermosa Beach clients head to the beach while we work.

Is there anything you won't remove?

We don't handle hazardous materials like chemicals, paint, or asbestos. We also can't remove items that require special permits. Everything else is fair game.
